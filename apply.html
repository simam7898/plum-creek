How do I apply?






Plum Creek Supportive Living offers an affordable alternative for seniors in
need of assistance to live independently. Individuals seeking an affordable
living option are encouraged to apply for residency. As a Supportive Living
Facility (SLF) certified by the Illinois Department of Healthcare and Family
Services, (HFS) qualified residents may have a portion of their services paid
for by the State of Illinois through the Medicaid Program.


Prospective residents are encouraged
to contact Plum Creek at 847-670-8080
or to make an appointment using the form
on this page to set up a tour of the facility.

Once a decision has been made that
Plum Creek is the place you would like
to reside, you will need to complete a
preliminary application.

Once the application is complete, our
director of nursing will contact you to
set up a pre-admission screening to
make sure that Plum Creek can meet
your individual needs.

After the pre-screening, you will be
referred to the Area Agency on Aging
who will conduct a Determination of
Need screening.

Throughout the process, our expert
staff will be available to guide you
along the way.

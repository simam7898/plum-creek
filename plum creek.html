Plum Creek is a warm and comfortable home for seniors 65 years and older. Inside our warm, caring environment we ensure the highest quality of life, while most importantly, maintaining your independence.





Our superior professional staff
are dedicated to the residents
they serve, promoting
independence, great nutrition, exciting activities and core
values for a happy, healthy
and active lifestyle in an oasis
you can call home.
Our affordable, all inclusive, monthly
price is geared towards seniors who
live on a budget.

Financial assistance is available for
those who qualify. With assets and
income under $2,000 your monthly
Social Security check affords you
this wonderful lifestyle.
